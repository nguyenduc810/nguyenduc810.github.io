Research interests
======

My research topics include **Multi-objective optimization(MOO), Multi-task learning(MTL)** involves training a model to perform multiple tasks simultaneously, which can lead to several challenges related to understanding the trade-offs between learning goals. 

I am also interested in architectural design for **Hypernetworks**, a type of network used to generate parameters for the target network. How to use Hypernetwork for MOO or MTL problems is also the knowledge I am researching.

In particular, I am also interested in topics about **Multi-objective recommender systems**. In addition to the objective of suggestion accuracy, we also have other objectives such as diversity, novelty.
